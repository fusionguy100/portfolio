Studying with CodeSignal

So for the past month or so I have been working with CodeSignal to improve my skills. While my college courses have taught me mostly Java and C++/ C, none of them have taught me Python, which I found interesting. So I took it upon myself to teach myself Python with CodeSignal. I love their platform as I find its a good way to learn and challenge yourself. I'm proud to say I'm on a 40 day streak so far and that I love Python. Compared to these other languages Python seems like a walk in the park and I feel like it might be my new favorite language. My goal is to be interview prepped and ready for some OA's for internships.
